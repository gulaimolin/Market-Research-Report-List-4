<p><p>Mechanical dust collectors are categorized into three main types based on their operation method: inertial dust collectors utilize the principle of inertia to separate dust particles from the air stream, centrifugal force dust collectors rely on centrifugal force to remove particles, and other types include electrostatic precipitators and scrubbers. These different mechanisms allow for efficient dust collection in various industries such as manufacturing, mining, and construction, contributing to improved air quality and worker safety.</p></p>
