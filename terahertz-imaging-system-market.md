<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging System market can be classified into two main types: Passive Terahertz Imaging and Active Terahertz Imaging. </p><p>Passive Terahertz Imaging involves the detection and visualization of natural terahertz radiation emitted by objects, without the need for an external radiation source. </p><p>On the other hand, Active Terahertz Imaging requires the use of an external terahertz radiation source to illuminate objects and capture the reflected signals for imaging purposes. </p><p>Both types of imaging systems have their own unique applications and advantages in various industries.</p></p>
