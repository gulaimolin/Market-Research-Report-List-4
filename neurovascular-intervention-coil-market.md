<p><p>Neurovascular intervention coils are devices used to treat vascular abnormalities such as aneurysms and blockages in the blood vessels of the brain. There are three main types of neurovascular intervention coils: coil-assist stents, flow diverter stents, and micro wire/catheter market. </p><p>-Coil-assist stents are coils that are placed inside a stent to provide additional support and prevent the coil from migrating.</p><p>-Flow diverter stents are devices used to redirect blood flow away from an aneurysm, reducing the risk of rupture.</p><p>-Micro wire/catheter market includes smaller coils and catheters used for precise delivery of coils to the targeted area within the brain.</p></p>
