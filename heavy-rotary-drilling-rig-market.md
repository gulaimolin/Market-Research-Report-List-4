<p><p>Heavy rotary drilling rigs are commonly classified into two main types: crawler and rubber-tired. Crawler drilling rigs are equipped with tracks that enable them to navigate rough and uneven terrains, making them suitable for working in challenging environments such as construction sites and mining operations. On the other hand, rubber-tired drilling rigs are mounted on wheels, providing them with greater mobility and flexibility to move quickly from one location to another. Both types of rigs have their own unique characteristics and advantages, catering to different market demands.</p></p>
