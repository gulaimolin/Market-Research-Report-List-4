<p><p>Automotive Multi Wheel Drive (AWD) Systems come in various types to cater to different driving needs. Part-Time AWD allows drivers to manually switch between 2-wheel and 4-wheel drive as needed for better traction. Full-Time AWD constantly sends power to all wheels, providing enhanced grip in all driving conditions. Automatic All-Wheel Drive systems utilize sensors to automatically engage all wheels when extra traction is needed. These different types of AWD systems offer drivers flexibility and control over their vehicle's performance in various road conditions.</p></p>
