<p><p>Chlorine Dioxide Analyzer market can be segmented into Amperometry Type and Photometry Type. Amperometry Type analyzers measure the concentration of chlorine dioxide by measuring the electric current produced during the oxidation-reduction reaction. Photometry Type analyzers, on the other hand, use light absorption to determine the concentration of chlorine dioxide. Both types offer accurate and reliable measurements of chlorine dioxide levels in water treatment processes, ensuring effective disinfection and water quality control.</p></p>
