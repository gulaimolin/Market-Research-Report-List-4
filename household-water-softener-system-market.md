<p><p>A Household Water Softener System is a device used to remove minerals such as calcium and magnesium from hard water, which can cause limescale buildup in pipes and appliances. Another trend is the growing popularity of salt-free water softeners, which use alternative technologies such as template-assisted crystallization to soften water without the need for salt. Both types of systems aim to improve water quality, reduce scale buildup, and extend the lifespan of household appliances.
