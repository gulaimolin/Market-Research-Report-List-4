Rotary Screw compressors are more suitable for continuous operation at high capacities, making them ideal for industrial applications. On the other hand, Reciprocating compressors are better suited for intermittent use at lower capacities, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations or applications where fluctuating output is common. Both types cater to different market needs and play a crucial role in providing temporary compressed air solutions for various industries.</p></p>
