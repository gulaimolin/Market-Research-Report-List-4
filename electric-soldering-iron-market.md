<p><p>Electric soldering iron market can be categorized into two main types: External Heated Soldering Iron and Internal Heated Soldering Iron. External Heated Soldering Irons have the heating element externally connected to the iron tip, allowing for quick and easy tip replacement. On the other hand, Internal Heated Soldering Irons have the heating element integrated within the iron tip itself, providing better heat transfer and improved soldering performance. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of users in the soldering industry.</p></p>
