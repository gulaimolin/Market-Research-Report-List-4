<p><p>An Inverter Microwave Oven is a type of microwave oven that uses inverter technology to control the power output for more precise cooking. Traditional microwave ovens use transformers to convert the incoming electricity into one or more high-voltage currents necessary to power the magnetron that generates the microwaves. In contrast, inverter microwave ovens utilize a different type of power supply that delivers a continuous stream of cooking power at all temperature levels. This results in more even cooking and allows for more controlled defrosting and reheating.</p><p>The Inverter Microwave Oven Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.80% during the forecast period.
