<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Antibody Library Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phage Display</li><li>Ribosome Display</li><li>Yeast Display</li><li>Mammalian Cell Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antibody library technologies offer a wide range of methods to display and identify antibodies for various applications. Phage display involves displaying antibodies on the surface of phages, while ribosome display utilizes ribosomes to link antibody genes with their corresponding proteins. Yeast display and mammalian cell display both involve displaying antibodies on the surface of yeast or mammalian cells, allowing for the selection of specific antibody fragments. These technologies provide powerful tools for discovering and optimizing antibodies for therapeutic, diagnostic, and research purposes.</p></p>
