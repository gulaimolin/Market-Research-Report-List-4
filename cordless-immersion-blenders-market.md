Medium duty immersion blenders are more robust and can handle more frequent use and tougher ingredients like nuts or frozen fruits. Heavy duty immersion blenders are the most powerful and are ideal for commercial kitchens or heavy-duty blending tasks such as pureeing soups or sauces in large quantities.</p></p>
