<p><ul><li>Blow Ventilation System</li><li>Suction Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car seat ventilation systems come in two main types: blow ventilation and suction ventilation. In blow ventilation systems, air is blown through small holes in the seat to keep the user cool and comfortable. On the other hand, suction ventilation systems work by sucking hot air away from the user, creating a cooling effect. Both types of ventilation systems are designed to improve comfort during long drives and can be found in various car seat models on the market.</p></p>
