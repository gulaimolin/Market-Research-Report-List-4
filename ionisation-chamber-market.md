Parallel Plate Ionisation Chambers are suitable for high-energy photon and electron beams. Thimble Type Ionisation Chambers are used for measuring beta and gamma radiation in air. Other types include spherical and rectangular chambers, each serving specific purposes in radiation detection and measurement.</p></p>
