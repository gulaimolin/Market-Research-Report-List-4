<p><ul><li>Single Core Cable</li><li>Multi Core Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Submarine Power Cable market is divided into two main types: Single Core Cable and Multi Core Cable. Single Core cables consist of a single conductor surrounded by insulation and a protective sheath, while Multi Core cables have multiple conductors within a single outer sheath. Single Core cables are often used for longer distance transmissions, while Multi Core cables are preferred for shorter distances and applications requiring higher power capacity. Both types play a critical role in connecting offshore renewable energy sources to onshore power grids.</p></p>
