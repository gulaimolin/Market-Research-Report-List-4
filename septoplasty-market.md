<p><p>Septoplasty is a surgical procedure performed to correct a deviated septum, improving nasal airflow. There are two main types of septoplasty procedures: Closed Septoplasty and Open Septoplasty. Closed Septoplasty is a minimally invasive procedure where incisions are made inside the nose, resulting in less scarring and quicker recovery time. Open Septoplasty involves making a small incision on the outside of the nose, allowing for better visibility and access to correct severe deformities. Both methods aim to straighten the nasal septum for better breathing and reduced symptoms.</p></p>
