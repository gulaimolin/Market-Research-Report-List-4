<p><p>Glass Lined Reactors come in three main types based on the design of the glass lining - AE Type, BE Type, and CE Type. AE Type reactors have a smooth enamel surface, providing good chemical resistance. BE Type reactors have a blue enamel coating and are suitable for highly corrosive environments. CE Type reactors, also known as enamel-lined reactors, have a harder, more abrasion-resistant coating for increased durability. Each type caters to different needs in terms of chemical resistance and durability in various industrial processes.</p></p>
