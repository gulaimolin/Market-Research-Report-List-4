<p><p>SSRs (Solid State Relays) are electronic switches that control power circuits using solid-state components. DIN Rail Mount SSRs are designed to be installed on standard DIN rails for efficient organization and space-saving. PCB Mount SSRs are integrated onto printed circuit boards for compact and integrated control solutions. Each market type caters to different installation requirements and applications.</p></p>
