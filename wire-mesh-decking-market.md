<p><p>Wire mesh decking is available in various types, including Flared Channel Decking and U-Channel Decking. Flared Channel Decking features flared channels on both ends for secure placement on beams, while U-Channel Decking has U-shaped channels that fit securely onto the beams. Flared Channel Decking is ideal for heavy-duty applications where extra support is needed, while U-Channel Decking is commonly used for lighter loads. Both types of wire mesh decking provide efficient storage solutions and ensure the safety of warehouse operations.</p></p>
