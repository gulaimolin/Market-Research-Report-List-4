<p><p>Contact-Type Deformation or Warpage Measurement System require physical contact with the surface being measured, typically using a probe or sensor. These systems provide accurate measurements but may cause damage to delicate materials. </p><p>Contactless Deformation or Warpage Measurement System use non-contact methods such as laser or optical sensors to measure surface deformations or warpage. These systems are non-invasive and safe for fragile materials, but may be less accurate compared to contact-type systems. Both types of systems are used in various industries for quality control and product development purposes.</p></p>
