<p><p>Robot Laser Cleaning Machine market offers two main types: Gas Laser and Solid Laser. Solid Laser cleaning machines use solid-state materials such as neodymium-doped crystals to generate the laser beam, providing higher beam quality and precision. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications for tasks such as rust removal, surface cleaning, and paint stripping.</p></p>
