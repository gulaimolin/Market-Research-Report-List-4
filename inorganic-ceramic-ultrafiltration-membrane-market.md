<p><p>Inorganic Ceramic Ultrafiltration Membrane market includes two main types: Tubular Membrane and Flat-sheet Membrane. Tubular Membrane is a cylindrical structure made of inorganic ceramic materials, offering high durability and better resistance to chemical degradation. On the other hand, Flat-sheet Membrane has a planar structure and provides a larger filtration area, making it ideal for applications requiring high flux and efficiency. Both types of membranes are widely used in various industries for water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food & beverage processing.</p></p>
