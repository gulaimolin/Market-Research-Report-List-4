<p><ul><li>IC Card</li><li>Non-IC Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Single Phase Electricity Smart Meters are divided into two main market types: IC Card and Non-IC Card. IC Card meters require users to purchase and insert a special card with credit to access electricity, providing a convenient and prepaid method for managing energy consumption. Non-IC Card meters, on the other hand, do not require a special card for access and instead use other methods for monitoring and billing electricity usage. Both types offer different features and benefits to cater to varying consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
