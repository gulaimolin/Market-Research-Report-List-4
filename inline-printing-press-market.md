<p><p>Inline printing presses come in two main types: manual and automatic. Manual printing presses require operators to manually feed in paper and adjust settings throughout the printing process. This type of press is suitable for small-scale printing operations. In contrast, automatic printing presses are equipped with sensors and automated mechanisms that handle paper feeding, ink distribution, and quality control. These presses are ideal for high-volume printing tasks and offer increased efficiency and consistency in the printing process.</p></p>
