<p><strong>The High Pressure Ball Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Remote Mounting</li><li>Direct Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High pressure ball valves can be categorized into two main types: Remote Mounting and Direct Mounting. Remote Mounting valves are designed to be installed away from the actuator, allowing for easy access and operation in hard-to-reach locations. Direct Mounting valves, on the other hand, are directly attached to the actuator, providing a more compact and integrated solution. Both types cater to different needs, with Remote Mounting offering flexibility and convenience, while Direct Mounting offers a more streamlined and efficient setup.</p></p>
