<p><p>The Wedge Wire Filter market consists of two main types: Tube Type Wedge Wire Filter and Screen Type Wedge Wire Filter. Tube Type filters are designed as cylindrical tubes with wedge-shaped wire wrapped around a support rod, providing efficient filtration for liquid and gas applications. On the other hand, Screen Type filters have flat screens with wedge-shaped wire welded onto them, offering excellent filtration for solid particles and high flow rates. Both types cater to different filtration needs and are widely used across various industries.</p></p>
