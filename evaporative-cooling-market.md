<p><p>Evaporative cooling market can be categorized into three main types - direct evaporative cooling, indirect evaporative cooling, and two-stage evaporative cooling. </p><p>Direct evaporative cooling involves the direct evaporation of water into the air to cool it down. Indirect evaporative cooling uses a heat exchanger to cool the air without adding moisture. Two-stage evaporative cooling combines both direct and indirect methods to achieve greater cooling efficiency. Each type has its own unique advantages and is suitable for different applications based on specific cooling requirements.</p></p>
