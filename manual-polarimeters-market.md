<p><p>Manual Polarimeters are available in three main types based on the number of wavelengths they use: Single Wavelength Polarimeters, Dual Wavelength Polarimeters, and Multiple Wavelength Polarimeters. Single Wavelength Polarimeters measure the rotation of light at a single wavelength, while Dual Wavelength Polarimeters use two wavelengths for increased accuracy. Multiple Wavelength Polarimeters offer even higher precision by using three or more wavelengths to measure the optical rotation of samples. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of users in various industries.</p></p>
