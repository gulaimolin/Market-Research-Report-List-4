<p><p>Split Flow Pump market can be categorized into two main types based on their power source: Electric and Hydraulic. Electric pumps use electricity to power the pump, making them suitable for indoor applications where access to electrical outlets is available. On the other hand, Hydraulic pumps use hydraulic fluid to generate pressure and power the pump, making them ideal for outdoor and heavy-duty applications where electricity may not be accessible. Both types of pumps offer reliable performance and efficiency in their respective applications.</p></p>
