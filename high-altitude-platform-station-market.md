<p><p>A High-Altitude Platform Station (HAPS) is a station, located in the Earth's stratosphere (approximately 17-22 km above sea level), that provides wireless communication and surveillance services. It is positioned higher than conventional ground-based towers, enabling broader coverage and improved communication capabilities. HAPS platforms can be stationary or mobile, powered by solar energy, and offer low-latency, high-capacity communication services.</p><p>The High-Altitude Platform Station Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period.
