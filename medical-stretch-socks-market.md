<p><p>Medical stretch socks come in various types, including gradient socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks are designed to apply pressure to the legs, with the highest pressure at the ankle and decreasing as it moves up the leg. This helps improve blood circulation and reduce swelling. Anti-embolism socks are specifically designed to prevent blood clots by applying consistent pressure to the legs. Both types of socks are beneficial for individuals who may be at risk for circulation issues or blood clots.</p></p>
