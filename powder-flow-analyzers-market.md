<p><p>Powder flow analyzers are used to analyze the flow properties of powders and granular materials. Two main types of analyzers are dynamic image analysis and static image analysis. Dynamic image analysis involves continuously monitoring and analyzing the movement of particles in real-time. Static image analysis captures a snapshot of particle distribution at a single moment in time. Both types of analyzers provide valuable information on particle size distribution, shape, and flow characteristics to optimize manufacturing processes in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food, and materials science.</p></p>
