<p><p>A turret truck is a type of forklift that is designed to operate in narrow aisle racking systems. It features a rotating turret mast that allows the operator to pick and store goods from both sides of the aisle without having to turn the entire truck around. This makes turret trucks ideal for high density storage warehouses where space is limited.</p><p>The turret truck market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period.
