<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Heat Source</li><li>Resistance Heat Source</li><li>Arc Heat Source</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric vacuum furnace market is divided into three main types based on heat sources - Electromagnetic, Resistance, and Arc. Electromagnetic heat source utilizes electromagnetic induction for heating materials, making it efficient and fast. Resistance heat source uses electrical resistance to generate heat, making it precise and controllable. Arc heat source involves creating an electric arc between two electrodes, producing high temperatures for melting and processing materials. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in industries such as metallurgy, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
