<p><p>Self-compacting concrete market can be classified into three main types: powder, viscosity, and combination. The powder market pertains to the use of mineral and chemical powdered ingredients to improve flowability and deformability of concrete mix. The viscosity market focuses on controlling the rheological properties of the concrete mixture to ensure proper flow and filling ability. The combination market combines both powder and viscosity techniques to create a self-compacting concrete mixture that is easy to work with and has excellent performance properties.</p></p>
