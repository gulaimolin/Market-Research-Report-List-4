<p><p>Resistance welding machines are categorized into three main types based on their level of automation: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual resistance welding machines require operators to manually control the welding process, making them ideal for smaller scale operations. Semi-automatic machines automate certain aspects of the welding process, such as electrode movement, while still requiring some manual input. Automatic resistance welding machines are fully automated, making them suitable for high-volume production where consistency and efficiency are key.</p></p>
