<p><p>Mining gas alarms are devices used to detect hazardous gases in mining environments for the safety of workers. There are two main types of mining gas alarms: stationary gas alarms and portable gas alarms. Stationary gas alarms are fixed in a specific location within the mine, continuously monitoring the air quality. Portable gas alarms, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be carried by miners as they move around the mine, providing real-time gas detection to ensure a safe working environment.</p></p>
