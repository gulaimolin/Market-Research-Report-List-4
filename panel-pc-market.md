<p><ul><li>Fan-Enabled Panel PC</li><li>Fan-Less Panel PC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Panel PCs are categorized into two main types based on their cooling systems: Fan-Enabled Panel PC and Fan-Less Panel PC. Fan-Enabled Panel PCs use internal fans to dissipate heat generated by the system, allowing for efficient cooling during high-performance operations. On the other hand, Fan-Less Panel PCs rely on passive cooling methods such as heat sinks and heat pipes to regulate temperature without the need for fans. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the application requirements, with Fan-Less Panel PCs offering silent operation and reduced maintenance needs.</p></p>
