<p><p>Electronics Nitrogen Generators are used to produce high purity nitrogen gas for various applications. There are three main types of nitrogen generators on the market: Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), Membrane, and Cryogenic Air. </p><p>PSA generators rely on the adsorption of oxygen on a solid surface to separate nitrogen from air. Membrane generators use selective permeability of membranes to separate nitrogen from air. Cryogenic generators cool air to very low temperatures to separate nitrogen from other gases through liquefaction and distillation processes. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
