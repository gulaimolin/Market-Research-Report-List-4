<p><p>Steel bending machines are available in three main types: manual bending machines, hydraulic steel bending machines, and CNC steel bending machines. Manual bending machines require human effort to operate, making them suitable for small-scale bending tasks. Hydraulic steel bending machines use hydraulic pressure to bend steel, providing more power and precision. CNC steel bending machines are the most advanced, using computer numerical control to automate the bending process for increased efficiency and accuracy. Each type of machine caters to different needs and budgets within the steel bending industry.</p></p>
