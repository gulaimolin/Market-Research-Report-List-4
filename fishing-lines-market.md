<p><p>Fishing lines market can be divided into two main types: braided and monofilament. Braided fishing lines are made of multiple synthetic fibers woven together, offering high strength and low stretch. They are ideal for fishing in heavy cover or deep waters. Monofilament lines are made of a single strand of nylon or other materials, offering good elasticity and flexibility. They are commonly used for light tackle fishing and casting. Both types have their own advantages and are popular choices among anglers for different fishing conditions.</p></p>
