<p><p>The Respiratory Protection Equipment Market includes two main types: Air-purifying Respirators and Supplied Air Respirators. Air-purifying Respirators are designed to filter out contaminants from the air before it is inhaled by the wearer. Supplied Air Respirators, on the other hand, deliver clean air from an external source to the wearer. Both types of respirators are crucial for protecting individuals in environments where air quality may be compromised, such as construction sites, chemical plants, or healthcare settings.</p></p>
