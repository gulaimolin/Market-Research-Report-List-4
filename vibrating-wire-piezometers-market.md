<p><p>Normal type vibrating wire piezometers are the traditional type that measure groundwater pressure by detecting changes in the resonant frequency of a vibrating wire. These are widely used in various applications for monitoring water levels. On the other hand, smart vibrating wire piezometers are equipped with advanced features like remote monitoring and data recording capabilities. They provide real-time data and alerts, making them suitable for more complex and large-scale monitoring projects. Both types serve the purpose of measuring groundwater pressure but smart piezometers offer additional benefits for advanced monitoring needs.</p></p>
