<p><p>Voltage Controlled Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillators (VCTCXOs) are classified into three main market types based on their output signal: Output PECL, Output CMOS, and Output SINEWAVE. Output PECL VCTCXOs provide a high-speed differential output signal, making them suitable for applications requiring fast data transfer. Output CMOS VCTCXOs produce a standard digital output signal, making them widely compatible with various electronic systems. Output SINEWAVE VCTCXOs generate a sinusoidal output signal, making them ideal for applications where a clean analog signal is required.</p></p>
