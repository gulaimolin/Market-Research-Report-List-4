<p><p>Synthetic compressor oils are classified into three main types based on their chemical composition: Polyalphaolefins (PAO), Polyalkylene Glycols (PAG), and Esters. PAO oils offer excellent stability and performance in a wide range of temperatures. PAG oils are known for their high viscosity index and compatibility with refrigerants. Esters are favored for their strong lubrication properties and resistance to oxidation. Each type has its unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different requirements in the compressor oil market.</p></p>
