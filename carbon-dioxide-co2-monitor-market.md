<p><p>The Carbon Dioxide (CO2) Monitor market consists of different types of monitors, including End-Tidal Carbon Dioxide (EtCO2) Monitors and Transcutaneous Carbon Dioxide (tcpCO2) Monitors. EtCO2 monitors measure the concentration of CO2 in exhaled breath, providing vital information for patients undergoing anesthesia or in critical care settings. On the other hand, tcpCO2 monitors measure CO2 levels through the skin, offering a non-invasive alternative for continuous monitoring in neonatal and pediatric patients. These different types of monitors cater to specific needs in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
