<p><p>Urban Rail Transit Market includes three main types: Metro Rail, Light Rail, and Monorail. Metro Rail systems operate underground, above ground, or a combination of both in dense urban areas. Light Rail systems typically run at street level or on elevated tracks, connecting urban and suburban areas. Monorail systems have trains that run on a single rail, often elevated, providing a unique and efficient mode of transportation in crowded cities. Each type offers different benefits based on the specific needs of the city and its residents.</p></p>
