<p><p>HVOF (High-Velocity Oxy-Fuel) equipment is available in two main types: liquid fuel and gas fuel. Liquid fuel type HVOF equipment utilizes liquid fuel such as kerosene or diesel to generate the necessary heat for the coating process. Gas fuel type HVOF equipment, on the other hand, relies on gases like hydrogen or propane for the same purpose. Both types of equipment provide high velocity spraying of coatings onto surfaces, resulting in enhanced performance and durability of the coated materials.</p></p>
