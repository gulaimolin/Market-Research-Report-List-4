Manual adjustable guards require manual adjustment based on the size and shape of the machine being guarded. On the other hand, automatic adjusting guards use sensors to detect the size of the machine and adjust accordingly. Both types provide protection for workers by preventing them from coming into contact with moving parts of machines. The market for these types of guards is driven by the need for safety in industrial settings.</p></p>
