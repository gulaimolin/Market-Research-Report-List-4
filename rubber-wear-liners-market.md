<p><p>Rubber wear liners are used in various industries to protect equipment from abrasion and impact damage. There are three main types of rubber wear liners - cylinder, cube, and shell. Cylinder wear liners are cylindrical in shape and are commonly used in chutes and hoppers to guide material flow. Cube wear liners are square or rectangular in shape and provide protection in high-wear areas. Shell wear liners are curved to fit the contours of equipment such as crushers and screens, providing comprehensive protection.</p></p>
