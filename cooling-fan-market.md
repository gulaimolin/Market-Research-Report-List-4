<p><p>Cooling fan market types can be categorized into Axial Fans, Centrifugal Fans, and Others. Axial fans are designed to move air along the axis of the fan, making them suitable for applications requiring high airflow. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, use a rotating impeller to create airflow perpendicular to the fan, making them ideal for applications requiring high pressure. The "Others" category typically includes specialty fans such as cross-flow fans, mixed-flow fans, and tangential fans, each offering unique performance characteristics for specific applications.</p></p>
