<p><strong>The Electron Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs)</li><li>Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electron microscopes are advanced imaging devices used for studying nanoscale structures. There are two main types in the market: Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs) and Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs). SEMs are used to create detailed surface images by scanning a focused electron beam over a sample. On the other hand, TEMs are used to study the internal structure of samples by transmitting electrons through them. Both types of electron microscopes play a crucial role in various scientific fields for research and analysis purposes.</p></p>
