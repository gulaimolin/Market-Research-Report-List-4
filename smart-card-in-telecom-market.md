<p><p>Smart cards are widely used in the telecom market for various purposes such as secure authentication, payment transactions, and access control. There are two main types of smart cards: contact cards and contactless cards. Contact cards require physical contact with a card reader for data transfer, while contactless cards use radio frequency technology for communication without physical contact. Both types of smart cards provide enhanced security and convenience, making them essential tools in the telecom industry for secure transactions and access control.</p></p>
