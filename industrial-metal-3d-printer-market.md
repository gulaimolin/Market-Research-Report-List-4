<p><strong>The Industrial Metal 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Metal 3D Printer Market consists of different types such as Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electronic Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powder to build up the desired object layer by layer. On the other hand, EBM uses an electron beam to melt and fuse metal powder in a vacuum chamber. Other types of metal 3D printing technologies include Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) and Binder Jetting. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
