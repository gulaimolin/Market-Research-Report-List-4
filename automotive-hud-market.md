<p><ul><li>Windshield Head-Up Display</li><li>Combiner Head-Up Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive HUD market types include Windshield Head-Up Display and Combiner Head-Up Display. Windshield HUD projects information directly onto the windshield, allowing drivers to view critical data without taking their eyes off the road. Combiner HUD uses a separate display screen that reflects information onto a transparent panel in the driver's line of sight. Both types aim to enhance driving safety and convenience by providing real-time information such as speed, navigation directions, and warnings without distracting the driver.</p></p>
