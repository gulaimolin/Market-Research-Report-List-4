<p><p>The Mercury Porosimeter market can be categorized into low-pressure, medium-pressure, and high-pressure types based on the pressure levels at which the porosimetry analysis is conducted. Low-pressure porosimetry typically utilizes pressures below 1 atm to analyze larger pore sizes, while medium-pressure porosimetry involves pressures between 1-5000 psi to analyze medium-sized pores. On the other hand, high-pressure porosimetry utilizes pressures exceeding 5000 psi to analyze small-sized pores. Each type of porosimeter offers unique capabilities and is suitable for different applications based on the pore sizes being analyzed.</p></p>
