<p><p>Hydrophilic and hydrophobic medical coatings are specialized coatings applied to medical devices to modify their surface properties, making them either water-attracting (hydrophilic) or water-repellent (hydrophobic). Hydrophilic coatings help reduce friction and improve lubricity, making devices easier to insert and navigate within the body. Hydrophobic coatings, on the other hand, are used to repel water and other liquids, preventing adhesion of biological materials on the device surface.</p><p>The global hydrophilic and hydrophobic medical coatings market is witnessing steady growth, driven by increasing demand for minimally invasive surgeries, technological advancements in coating materials, and rising prevalence of chronic diseases. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period.</p><p>Key trends in the hydrophilic and hydrophobic medical coatings market include the development of nanotechnology-based coatings, growing adoption of anti-microbial coatings to prevent infections, and rising demand for drug-eluting coatings for controlled drug release.
