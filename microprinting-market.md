<p><p>Microprinting Market offers various types of security features for document and product authentication. UV Invisible Marking utilizes special inks that are only visible under ultraviolet light. Special Inks are designed to be difficult to replicate and can include color-changing or fluorescent properties. Infrared Ink Marking uses inks that are only visible under infrared light. Magnetic Ink contains magnetic particles for security verification. Micro-Embossing creates tiny raised patterns for added security. Other market types include holograms, watermarks, and security threads for comprehensive counterfeit protection.</p></p>
