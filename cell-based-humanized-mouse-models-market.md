<p><p>Cell-based humanized mouse models are utilized in research to mimic human immune responses and disease progression. The CD34 humanized mouse model involves engrafting human hematopoietic stem cells, resulting in a fully functional human immune system. PBMC humanized mouse models involve injecting human peripheral blood mononuclear cells to study specific immune responses. BLT humanized mouse models are created by engrafting human bone marrow, liver, and thymus tissues, allowing the study of various immune functions and disease mechanisms. These models offer valuable insights into human biology and can aid in drug development and personalized medicine.</p></p>
