<p><p>Fiberglass molds are categorized based on the type of resin used in their construction. Epoxy resin fiberglass molds offer high strength and resistance to corrosion, making them suitable for applications requiring durability. Vinyl ester resin fiberglass molds provide enhanced flexibility and chemical resistance, making them ideal for applications in harsh environments. Polyester resin fiberglass molds are cost-effective and offer good dimensional stability, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. Each type of resin offers unique properties to cater to different market needs.</p></p>
