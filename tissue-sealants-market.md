<p><p>Tissue sealants are materials used to securely close or seal wounds or incisions during surgeries. There are two main types of tissue sealants in the market - Fibrin Sealants and Synthetic Sealants. Fibrin sealants are made from blood proteins that help in clotting and are effective in sealing tissues. Synthetic sealants, on the other hand, are man-made materials that mimic the body's natural clotting process. Both types of sealants are used in various medical procedures to provide hemostasis and promote healing.</p></p>
