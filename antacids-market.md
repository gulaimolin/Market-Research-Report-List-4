Non-Systemic Antacids work by neutralizing stomach acid and providing quick relief, but they do not enter the bloodstream. Systemic Antacids, on the other hand, are absorbed into the bloodstream and can provide longer-lasting relief by reducing the production of stomach acid. Both types of antacids are available in various forms such as tablets, liquids, and chews to cater to different preferences and needs of consumers in the market.</p></p>
