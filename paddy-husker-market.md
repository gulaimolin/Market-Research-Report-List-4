<p><p>Paddy huskers can be categorized into two main types in the market: semi-pneumatic and pneumatic. Semi-pneumatic paddy huskers are equipped with partially pneumatic components, helping to remove the husk from the paddy grains efficiently. On the other hand, pneumatic paddy huskers are completely powered by pneumatic systems, providing a more advanced and automated husking process. Both types offer efficient husking solutions for rice mill owners, with pneumatic huskers being more high-end and costly compared to semi-pneumatic ones.</p></p>
