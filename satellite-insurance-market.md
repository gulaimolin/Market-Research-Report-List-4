<p><strong>The Satellite Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground risk</li><li>Satellite risk</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ground risk insurance in the satellite industry covers damages or losses that occur while the satellite is on the ground, such as during transportation or testing. This type of insurance is essential for mitigating financial risks associated with accidents or malfunctions before the satellite is launched. On the other hand, satellite risk insurance covers damages or losses that occur once the satellite is in orbit, such as collisions with space debris or technical failures. Both types of insurance are crucial for protecting investments in the satellite industry.</p></p>
