<p><p>Laboratory TDS Meters market is divided into three types based on the number of channels they have: Single Channel, Dual Channel, and Multi Channel. Single Channel meters can only measure one sample at a time, making them suitable for small-scale applications. Dual Channel meters allow for the simultaneous measurement of two samples, increasing efficiency in medium-sized laboratories. Multi Channel meters can measure multiple samples at once, making them ideal for high-throughput testing in large laboratories. Each type caters to different laboratory needs and scale of operations.</p></p>
