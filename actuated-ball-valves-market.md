<p><p>Actuated ball valves are available in various types, such as electrically actuated ball valves, pneumatically actuated ball valves, and air actuated ball valves. Electrically actuated ball valves are operated by an electric motor, providing precise control and automation. Pneumatically actuated ball valves use compressed air to control the valve's position, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Air actuated ball valves use air pressure to control the valve's opening and closing, offering a cost-effective and efficient solution for fluid control systems.</p></p>
