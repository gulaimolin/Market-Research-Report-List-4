Piston Type accumulators utilize a moving piston to separate the gas and fluid, offering high energy storage efficiency. The Others segment may include bladder type accumulators or variations of the two main types. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in various industries requiring reliable energy storage solutions.</p></p>
