<p><p>Bricking machines can be classified into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic bricking machines require minimal human intervention, as they are capable of performing all functions automatically, from feeding raw materials to forming and cutting bricks. On the other hand, semi-automatic bricking machines require some manual input and monitoring, such as feeding raw materials and removing finished bricks. Both types of machines are widely used in the construction industry for efficiently producing bricks of various sizes and shapes.</p></p>
