Gelatin excipients are derived from animal collagen and are commonly used in encapsulating drugs. Polymers such as cellulose derivatives and acrylic polymers create a barrier for controlled drug release. Minerals like magnesium stearate improve tablet cohesiveness. Sugars like lactose act as fillers. Alcohol helps in solubilization of drugs. Chitosan, a natural polymer, enhances drug delivery efficiency.</p></p>
