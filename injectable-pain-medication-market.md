<p><p>Injectable pain medication market consists of two main types - Nonsteroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) and Central Analgesics. NSAIDs work by reducing inflammation and pain by blocking certain enzymes in the body. They are commonly used for conditions like arthritis and menstrual pain. Central analgesics, on the other hand, work by targeting the central nervous system to relieve pain. They are often used for more severe pain, such as post-operative pain or cancer-related pain. Both types of medications are important in managing acute and chronic pain.</p></p>
