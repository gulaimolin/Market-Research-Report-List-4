<p><p>Dental phosphor storage plates (PSP) system is an imaging technology used in dentistry that allows for digital x-rays to be taken without the use of traditional film. The PSP system consists of thin, flexible plates coated with phosphor materials that record x-ray images when activated by exposure to radiation. These images can then be viewed instantly on a computer screen, providing dentists with high-quality diagnostic images while reducing the need for chemical processing and storage space required for traditional x-ray film.</p><p>The Dental PSP (Phosphor Storage Plates) System Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 6.1% during the forecast period.
