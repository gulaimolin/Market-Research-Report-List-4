<p><p>Parts washing cabinets are used to clean automotive, industrial, and manufacturing components. They come in two main types: solvent-based and aqueous-based. Solvent-based cabinets use petroleum-based chemicals to remove grease, oil, and grime from parts. On the other hand, aqueous-based cabinets use water-based detergents to clean parts without the use of harsh chemicals. Both types of cabinets are widely used in various industries for efficient and thorough cleaning of parts to ensure optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
