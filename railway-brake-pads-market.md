<p><p>Railway brake pads can be categorized into two main types: composite brake pads and powder metallurgy brake pads. Composite brake pads are made from a combination of materials such as rubber, metals, and fiberglass, offering a balance of performance, noise reduction, and durability. On the other hand, powder metallurgy brake pads are made from metal powders that are compacted and sintered together, providing excellent braking performance and wear resistance. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient braking in railway systems.</p></p>
