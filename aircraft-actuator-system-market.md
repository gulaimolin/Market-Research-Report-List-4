<p><p>Aircraft actuator systems are essential components that control the movement of various parts of an aircraft. There are two main types of actuator systems in the market - Electro-Hydraulic Actuators and Electro-Mechanical Actuators. </p><p>Electro-Hydraulic Actuators use a combination of hydraulic and electric power to generate motion, providing high force capabilities and precise control. On the other hand, Electro-Mechanical Actuators rely solely on electric power for operation, offering quieter and more efficient movement but with lower force capacities compared to their hydraulic counterparts.</p></p>
