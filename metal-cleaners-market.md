<p><p>Metal cleaners market can be divided into two main types - alkaline cleaners and acid cleaners. Alkaline cleaners are used for removing heavy oils, greases, carbon deposits, and other organic contaminants from metal surfaces. They are effective for cleaning aluminum, stainless steel, and other metals that are sensitive to acidic solutions. Acid cleaners, on the other hand, are used for removing scales, rust, and mineral deposits from metal surfaces. They are commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
