<p><p>Spin welders are classified into three main market types based on their level of automation: manual welders, semiautomated welders, and fully automated welders. Manual welders require operators to manually load and unload parts for welding. Semiautomated welders streamline the process by automating some aspects, such as part rotation or clamping. Fully automated welders require minimal operator intervention, as they are capable of loading parts, welding them, and unloading them without human assistance. Each market type offers different levels of efficiency and productivity for various manufacturing needs.</p></p>
