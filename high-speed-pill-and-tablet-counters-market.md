<p><p>High Speed Pill and Tablet Counters come in two main types: Automatic and Semi-automatic. Automatic counters are fully automated machines that are capable of counting large quantities of pills or tablets quickly and accurately. They are ideal for high volume production environments. Semi-automatic counters, on the other hand, require some manual intervention to operate, such as placing the containers for filling or removing filled containers. They are more suitable for smaller scale operations or when frequent changeovers are required. Both types offer efficient solutions for pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries.</p></p>
