<p><ul><li>Float-Feed Carburetor</li><li>Diaphragm Carburetor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Float-feed carburetors use a float mechanism to control the fuel level in the carburetor bowl, regulating the amount of fuel entering the engine. This type of carburetor is commonly found in older vehicles and small engines. Diaphragm carburetors, on the other hand, use a flexible diaphragm to control the fuel flow, allowing for more precise adjustment. This type of carburetor is often used in newer small engines and equipment due to its efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
