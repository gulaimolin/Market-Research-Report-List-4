<p><strong>The Cryorefrigerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gifford-Mcmahon Cryorefrigerators</li><li>Pulse-Tube Cryorefrigerators</li><li>Stirling Cryorefrigerators</li><li>Joule-Thomson Cryorefrigerators</li><li>Brayton Cryorefrigerators</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryorefrigerators are classified into different types based on their mechanism of operation. Gifford-Mcmahon Cryorefrigerators use a piston and displacer mechanism. Pulse-Tube Cryorefrigerators operate using a pulse-tube that generates cooling effects. Stirling Cryorefrigerators utilize a Stirling cycle for refrigeration. Joule-Thomson Cryorefrigerators rely on the Joule-Thomson effect for cooling. Brayton Cryorefrigerators employ a Brayton cycle to achieve refrigeration. Other types of cryorefrigerators may include sorption refrigerators, dilution refrigerators, and adiabatic demagnetization refrigerators. Each type of cryorefrigerator offers unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
