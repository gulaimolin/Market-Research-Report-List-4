<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Beam Profiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Camera Based</li><li>Scanning Split</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Beam Profiler market is divided into two main types: Camera Based and Scanning Split. Camera Based profilers use a camera sensor to capture an image of the laser beam for analysis, offering real-time measurements and high spatial resolution. On the other hand, Scanning Split profilers use a moving slit to scan across the beam, providing detailed information about beam intensity and size. Both types cater to different needs and applications within industries such as research, manufacturing, and healthcare.</p></p>
