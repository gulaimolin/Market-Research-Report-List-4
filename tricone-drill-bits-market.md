<p><strong>The Tricone Drill Bits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TCI Drill Bits</li><li>Milled Tooth Drill Bits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tricone drill bits are commonly categorized into two main types: TCI (Tungsten Carbide Insert) drill bits and Milled Tooth drill bits. TCI drill bits are equipped with durable Tungsten Carbide inserts that are designed to withstand harsh drilling conditions, making them suitable for hard rock formations. On the other hand, Milled Tooth drill bits have steel teeth that are ideal for softer formations. Both types of tricone drill bits are widely used in the oil and gas industry for efficient drilling operations.</p></p>
