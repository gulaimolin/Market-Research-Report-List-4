<p><p>The alkylbenzene market is divided into two main types: linear alkylbenzene and branched alkylbenzene. Linear alkylbenzene is a straight-chain version of the compound, known for its excellent cleaning properties and biodegradability, making it a popular choice in the detergent industry. Branched alkylbenzene, on the other hand, has a more complex structure with branching off the main chain, providing improved stability and resistance to breakdown, making it suitable for use in lubricants, fuels, and other industrial applications.</p></p>
