<p><p>Research steam sterilizers are used in various industries for sterilizing equipment and tools. There are three main types of steam sterilization cycles: Gravity Cycle, SFPP Cycle, and Pre-Vac Cycle. Gravity Cycle uses gravity to remove air from the chamber, SFPP Cycle uses a series of pulses to remove air, and Pre-Vac Cycle uses a pre-vacuum phase to remove air before steam is injected. Each type offers different advantages depending on the specific needs of the sterilization process.</p></p>
