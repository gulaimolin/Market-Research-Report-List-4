<p><p>Warehouse guard rail market offers two main types: double rail and single rail. Double rail guard rail systems consist of two horizontal rails connected by vertical posts, offering maximum protection from impacts. Single rail systems, on the other hand, feature a single horizontal rail supported by vertical posts, providing a cost-effective solution for lower impact zones. Both types serve the purpose of protecting infrastructure, equipment, and personnel in industrial environments, with the choice depending on the level of protection required and budget constraints.</p></p>
