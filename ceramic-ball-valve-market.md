<p><p>Ceramic ball valves are classified into different types based on their operation mechanisms. Manual operated ceramic ball valves are operated by hand, while electric operated valves are controlled by an electric motor. Pneumatic operated valves use compressed air to function. Other types of ceramic ball valves may include thermal or magnetic operated valves. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for specific applications, depending on the required level of precision, control, and automation.</p></p>
