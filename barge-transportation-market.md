<p><p>Barge transportation market is categorized into three main types: open barge, covered barge, and tank barge. Open barge refers to a barge without a roof, suitable for transporting bulk goods such as coal or aggregates. Covered barge, on the other hand, is equipped with a roof to protect cargo from the elements, commonly used for transporting sensitive goods like grains or chemicals. Tank barge is designed specifically for transporting liquid cargo such as oil or chemicals in a safe and efficient manner.</p></p>
