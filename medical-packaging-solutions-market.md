<p><strong>The Medical Packaging Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Packaging</li><li>Secondary Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Primary packaging refers to the packaging that comes into direct contact with the medical product, such as vials, bottles, or blister packs. This type of packaging is designed to protect the product from contamination, light, and moisture. Secondary packaging, on the other hand, includes outer packaging like cartons or boxes that hold the primary packaging. Secondary packaging is often used for branding, information, and transportation purposes. Both primary and secondary packaging play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and integrity of medical products.</p></p>
