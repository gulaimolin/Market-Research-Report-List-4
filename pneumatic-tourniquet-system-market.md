<p><ul><li>Single Bladder Tourniquet</li><li>Dual Bladder Tourniquet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic tourniquet systems are divided into two main types: Single Bladder Tourniquets and Dual Bladder Tourniquets. Single Bladder Tourniquets consist of one chamber that inflates around the limb to occlude blood flow, while Dual Bladder Tourniquets have two chambers that can independently control pressure in different parts of the limb. Both types are commonly used in medical procedures to temporarily restrict blood flow for surgeries or other interventions. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs of the procedure and the patient.</p></p>
