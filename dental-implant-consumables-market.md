<p><p>The dental implant consumables market is categorized into four main types: Implants, Abutment, Fixtures, and Others. Implants are the actual artificial tooth roots that are surgically placed into the jawbone. Abutments are the connecting elements that support and secure the crown to the implant. Fixtures refer to temporary components used during the implant procedure. The "Others" category includes various consumables such as bone grafts, membranes, and healing abutments. Each type plays a crucial role in the successful placement and function of dental implants.</p></p>
