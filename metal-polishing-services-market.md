<p><p>Metal polishing services can be categorized into three main types based on the method used: mechanical, chemistry, and electrochemistry. Mechanical polishing involves using abrasives to physically scrub and smooth the metal surface. Chemistry polishing utilizes chemical compounds to dissolve and remove imperfections from the surface. Electrochemistry polishing involves using an electrical current to enhance the chemical reactions and further polish the metal. Each market type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the metal being polished.</p></p>
