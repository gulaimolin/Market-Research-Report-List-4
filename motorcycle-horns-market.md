<p><p>The motorcycle horns market includes two main types: air horns and electric horns. Air horns are louder and produce a deeper sound by using compressed air to create a powerful honking noise. Electric horns, on the other hand, use electricity to create a high-pitched honk. Both types are popular among motorcycle riders for alerting other drivers and pedestrians on the road. The choice between air horns and electric horns often comes down to personal preference and the desired level of sound output.</p></p>
