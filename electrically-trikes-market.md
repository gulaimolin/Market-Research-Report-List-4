<p><p>Electrically trikes market can be divided into two main types based on motor placement - side wheel motors and center motors. Side wheel motors are located on either side of the rear wheels, providing stability and better traction. Center motors are positioned in the center of the trike frame, offering a more balanced distribution of weight and power delivery. Each type has its own advantages and is preferred by different consumers based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
