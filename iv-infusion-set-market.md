<p><ul><li>Venting Type</li><li>Closed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IV Infusion Set Market is segmented into two types: Venting Type and Closed Type. Venting Type infusion sets have a vent to allow air to enter the vial, preventing negative pressure buildup. Closed Type infusion sets do not have a vent, maintaining a closed system to prevent air contamination. The Venting Type market focuses on providing efficient air flow during infusion, while the Closed Type market emphasizes on maintaining a sterile environment during the entire infusion process.</p></p>
