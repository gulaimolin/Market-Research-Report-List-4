<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photomask Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor photomask inspection equipment market offers two main types of inspection methods: Die to Die (DD) method and Die to Database (DB) method. In the Die to Die method, two identical dies on the same photomask are compared to detect defects. On the other hand, the Die to Database method compares the die on the photomask with the design database to find any discrepancies. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor photomasks used in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
