<p><p>Inspection microscopes are categorized into two main types: binocular microscopes and trinocular microscopes. Binocular microscopes have two eyepieces, allowing for viewing with both eyes simultaneously. They are commonly used for routine inspection tasks. Trinocular microscopes, on the other hand, have three eyepieces, with the third being used for camera integration or additional accessories. Trinocular microscopes are typically preferred for more advanced applications such as photography, video recording, or digital image processing.</p></p>
