<p><ul><li>Adjustable</li><li>In-line Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio attenuators are widely used in various audio systems to control the volume levels. There are two main types of audio attenuators - adjustable and in-line. Adjustable attenuators allow users to manually adjust the volume levels to their desired setting, providing more flexibility and control. In-line attenuators, on the other hand, are fixed volume control devices that are inserted between audio sources and output devices to reduce or attenuate the sound level. Both types are essential in maintaining optimal audio quality and preventing distortion in audio systems.</p></p>
