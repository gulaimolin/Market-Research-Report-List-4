AC solid state relays are designed to control alternating current loads, while DC solid state relays are used for direct current loads. Both types offer advantages such as fast switching speeds, long lifespan, and improved reliability compared to traditional electromechanical relays. These SSRs are commonly used in industrial automation, process control, and power distribution systems.</p></p>
