<p><p>Single wafer processing equipment market can be segmented into three types- manual, semiautomatic, and automatic. In the manual process, operators are completely responsible for executing each step of the wafer processing. Semiautomatic equipment involves a combination of manual and automated processes, with operators controlling some steps and machines handling others. Automatic equipment, on the other hand, is fully automated with minimal human intervention, resulting in higher efficiency and productivity. Each type offers different levels of automation and control for wafer processing.</p></p>
