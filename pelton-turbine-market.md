<p><ul><li>Single Nozzle</li><li>Twin-Nozzle</li><li>Multi-Nozzle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pelton Turbines are commonly categorized into three market types based on the number of nozzles they feature: Single Nozzle, Twin-Nozzle, and Multi-Nozzle. Single Nozzle Pelton Turbines are the most basic and efficient, ideal for low to medium head applications. Twin-Nozzle Pelton Turbines are designed for medium head applications, offering improved performance compared to Single Nozzle turbines. Multi-Nozzle Pelton Turbines are used for high head applications, maximizing energy conversion by utilizing multiple nozzles for increased power production.</p></p>
