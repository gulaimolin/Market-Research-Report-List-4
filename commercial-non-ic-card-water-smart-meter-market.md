<p><p>Commercial Non-IC Card Water Smart Meters are available in two main market types: Single Phase and Three Phase. Single Phase meters are designed for smaller commercial establishments with lower power consumption, while Three Phase meters are intended for larger commercial establishments with higher power consumption. Single Phase meters typically measure electricity usage for a single circuit, whereas Three Phase meters measure electricity usage for three separate circuits, making them suitable for more complex electrical systems. Both types of meters are essential for effectively monitoring and managing water usage in commercial settings.</p></p>
