<p><p>Aircraft Machined Components Market is comprised of two main types: Milled Parts and Turned Parts. Milled parts are created by removing material from a workpiece using a rotating cutter, resulting in complex shapes and high precision. Turned parts are produced by rotating a workpiece on a lathe while a cutting tool removes material, resulting in cylindrical or conical shapes. Both types play a critical role in the aerospace industry, providing essential components for aircraft construction and maintenance.</p></p>
