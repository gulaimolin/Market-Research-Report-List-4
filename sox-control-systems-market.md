<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The SOx Control Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR) Reaction</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Reaction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR) Reaction is a type of SOx control system that involves injecting ammonia or urea into the combustion gases to reduce nitrogen oxides. On the other hand, Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Reaction is a system that uses a catalyst to convert nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and water in the presence of ammonia. Both systems are effective at reducing emissions of sulfur oxides and nitrogen oxides, improving air quality and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
