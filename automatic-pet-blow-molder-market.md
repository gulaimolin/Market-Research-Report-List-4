<p><p>The Automatic PET Blow Molder market consists of three main types: Extrusion Blow Molder, Injection Blow Molder, and Stretch Blow Molder. Extrusion Blow Molders use a continuous extrusion process to create hollow plastic objects. Injection Blow Molders inject hot liquid plastic into a mold to produce bottles and containers with precise dimensions. Stretch Blow Molders heat preforms and stretch them into shape using a mold. Each type of molder offers unique advantages for producing different shapes and sizes of PET containers.</p></p>
