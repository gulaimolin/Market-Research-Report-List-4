<p><p>Fiber optic rings are networks used to transmit data through optical fibers in a closed loop configuration. There are two main types of fiber optic rings: single-mode and multimode. Single-mode fiber optic rings are designed for long-distance, high-speed data transmission with a small core size, while multimode fiber optic rings are used for shorter distances with larger core sizes. Both types of fiber optic rings play a crucial role in telecommunications, networking, and data transmission industries.</p></p>
