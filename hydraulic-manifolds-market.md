<p><p>Hydraulic manifolds are components used in hydraulic systems to regulate fluid flow and pressure. There are two main types of hydraulic manifolds: Mono-Block Manifolds and Modular Manifold Blocks. Mono-Block manifolds are one-piece structures with integrated channels for fluid flow, while Modular Manifold Blocks are made up of multiple individual blocks that can be combined and connected to create a custom manifold system. Both types offer different benefits in terms of design flexibility, ease of installation, and overall system efficiency.</p></p>
