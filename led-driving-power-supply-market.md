<p><p>LED driving power supply market can be divided into two main types: External Power Supply and Built-in Power Supply. In the External Power Supply market, the power supply unit is separate from the LED lighting fixture, allowing for easier replacement and maintenance. On the other hand, the Built-in Power Supply market integrates the power supply unit directly into the LED fixture, reducing the overall size and potentially lowering costs. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements of customers in the LED lighting industry.</p></p>
