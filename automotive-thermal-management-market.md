<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Thermal Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Transmission Warmup</li><li>Exhaust Gas Recirculation(EGR)</li><li>Engine Thermal Mass Reduction</li><li>Reduced HVAC System Loading</li><li>Other Technologies</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive thermal management market involves various types of technologies to efficiently manage heat in vehicles. Active transmission warmup helps reduce emissions by warming up the transmission quickly. Exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) system reduces nitrogen oxide emissions by recirculating a portion of exhaust gases. Engine thermal mass reduction decreases the weight of engine components to improve fuel efficiency. Reduced HVAC system loading technology minimizes the load on the vehicle's heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system. Other technologies include heat exchangers, electric coolants, and variable displacement compressors.</p></p>
