<p><ul><li>2D Cell Culture System</li><li>3D Cell Culture System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>2D cell culture system involves growing cells on a flat surface, such as a petri dish, while 3D cell culture system incorporates a more complex environment that mimics the natural three-dimensional structure of tissues in the body. The 2D system is simpler and more cost-effective, making it suitable for basic research, drug screening, and toxicity testing. On the other hand, the 3D system better replicates the in vivo environment, allowing for more accurate modeling of disease states and drug responses. Both systems have their own advantages and applications in cell culture research.</p></p>
