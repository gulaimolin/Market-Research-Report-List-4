<p><ul><li>Constant Temperature</li><li>Variable Temperature</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Constant temperature infusion heaters maintain a consistent heating level throughout the infusion process, ensuring a stable and accurate temperature for medical fluids. On the other hand, variable temperature infusion heaters allow users to adjust the heating level based on specific requirements or preferences. While constant temperature heaters are ideal for precise applications where temperature control is critical, variable temperature heaters offer more flexibility and customization options. Both types cater to the diverse needs of medical professionals and patients in different clinical settings.</p></p>
