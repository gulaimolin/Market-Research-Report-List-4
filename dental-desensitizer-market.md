<p><ul><li>Fluoride-containing Dental Desensitizer</li><li>Fluoride-free dental desensitizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental desensitizers are products used to treat tooth sensitivity. Fluoride-containing dental desensitizers contain fluoride, which helps to strengthen the enamel and reduce sensitivity. On the other hand, fluoride-free dental desensitizers do not contain fluoride but use other ingredients like potassium nitrate or calcium phosphate to block pain signals in the teeth. Both types of desensitizers are effective in reducing tooth sensitivity, with fluoride-free options being suitable for individuals who may be sensitive to fluoride or prefer fluoride-free products.</p></p>
