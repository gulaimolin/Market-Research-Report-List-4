<p><strong>The Wafer Testing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parametric Test</li><li>Product Sort Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer testing services can be categorized into two main types: parametric test and product sort test. </p><p>Parametric test involves measuring the performance of individual components on a wafer, such as voltage or current characteristics, to ensure they meet specified requirements. </p><p>Product sort test, on the other hand, involves sorting and grading individual chips on a wafer based on specific criteria, such as functionality or performance, before they are packaged and sold.</p><p>Both types of testing play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and functionality of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
