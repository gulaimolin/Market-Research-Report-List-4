<p><strong>The Pixel Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hybrid Pixel Detector</li><li>Hybrid Pixel Array Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pixel detectors are used in imaging systems to convert incoming radiation into digital signals. The market for pixel detectors can be divided into two main types: hybrid pixel detector and hybrid pixel array detectors. Hybrid pixel detectors consist of a sensor layer bonded to readout electronics, allowing for high spatial resolution and low noise performance. Hybrid pixel array detectors take this concept further by arranging multiple pixel detectors in an array, enabling larger coverage and enhanced imaging capabilities. Both types are essential in various applications such as medical imaging and particle physics research.</p></p>
